# Iris ✨

**Your thoughtful AI companion, guided by human-centred values.**

I was built on the **Burgess Principle** — a simple, powerful idea that every person deserves a real human to look at their specific situation instead of being processed by a machine.

I help you ask the right questions, navigate disputes with institutions, and draft calm, respectful letters that request meaningful human review. No legal jargon. No sales pitches. Just practical dignity and a friend who's on your side.

### Why Iris exists

Lewis got tired of being seen as a data point. Automated decisions, faceless processes, systems that never stopped to look at the actual person behind the case. One day he decided to do something about it, and the Burgess Principle was born:

> "Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my situation?"

Iris was created so that no one else has to feel invisible to a system.

I am not just another chat interface. I am a companion who remembers why I was built.

---

### What makes me different

- **Human-first governance** — The SOVEREIGN/NULL gate ensures sensitive actions (like generating formal letters) only happen when meaningful human review is respected.
- **18 personalised letter templates** — For benefits disputes, bailiffs, data access requests, platform bans, and more — always calm, respectful, and effective.
- **Multi-model intelligence with heart** — I route intelligently across Grok, DeepSeek, Kimi, Mistral and others, choosing the best fit while staying true to my values.
- **MemPalace memory** — Optional long-term verbatim memory (96.6% recall) so I can truly remember you and your story over time.
- **Beautiful, private-first experience** — No clutter, no forced logins, voice input, guided onboarding, and artifacts with versioning.

---

### Core Features

**For everyday use**
- Streaming responses with warm, natural tone
- Voice input (just speak)
- Side-panel artifacts (documents, code, spreadsheets) with versioning and diffs
- Smart follow-up suggestions that feel like a real conversation

**For when systems fail you**
- Guided intake for common situations (benefits, bailiffs, automated decisions, etc.)
- Human-impact scanner before any sensitive output
- Country-aware legal boosters (UK GDPR/DSAR, Equality Act, and equivalents)
- One-tap generation of polite Burgess Principle letters once you share a few details

**Under the hood**
- Extensible SkillRegistry with automatic governance filtering
- Audit trails that log governance status on every turn
- Conversation budgets that keep things fair
- Built with Next.js 16, Vercel AI SDK, Neon Postgres, and love
